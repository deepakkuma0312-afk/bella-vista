About Bella Vesta

Our Story

Founded in 1985 by Rosel Wood.Bella Vista is an Italian restaurant located in the heart of the city, offering a warm atmosphere with authentic Italian charm.

The restaurant serves classic Italian dishes including pasta, pizza, risotto, and desserts, prepared with fresh ingredients and traditional recipes.
